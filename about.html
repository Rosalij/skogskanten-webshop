About Skogskanten

Skogskanten Creations makes handcrafted wooden accessories made by Rosali Johansson.



Our designs are thoughtfully created to ensure a minimal environmental footprint, with making your favorite accessiories out of natural wooden material. We offer free shipping within all of Sweden, while also shipping orders worldwide.


This is a fictive webshop by Rosali Johansson made for the course "Graphic techniques for the web", Mid Sweden University. The website is made with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and JSON, with image and video editing in Photoshop, Squoosh, Clipchamp and Handbrake.
